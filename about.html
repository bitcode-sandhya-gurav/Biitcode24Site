About Us

Biitcode / About Us

We are one of the most trusted partners in Information Technology & HR Services.

Trusted by Worlwide Business

More than 90,000+ teams are using Biitcode

Biitcode is a Pune-based Information technology company specializing in software development and recruitment services. Our company is established in January 2020, and since then we are consistently delivering innovative solutions with a client-centric approach.

Our Founder's decades of enriched experience in IT have equipped us to deliver excellent, trustworthy, and innovative solutions to our clients. Our comprehensive range of services includes application development, web, mobile app development, ERP development, software support, as well as recruitment & placement, staffing, and payroll services in India & throughout the world.

Our commitment is to deliver cutting-edge technology solutions and professional HR services tailored to meet the unique requirements of each client. Our dedicated team of experts strives to exceed expectations and ensure client satisfaction at every step of the way.

At Biitcode, we also work as a consultant & provide training to help businesses maximize their productivity. Whether you need custom software solutions, assistance with talent acquisition, or guidance for your business processes, we are your trusted partner for success in the digital world.
